Responsible Play

At Oz Pickz, we believe playing the lottery should be fun, exciting, and safe. Our goal is to provide information and results in a way that encourages mindful participation and helps prevent gambling-related harm.

Our Commitment

We promote responsible play by:

  • Encouraging players to set personal spending limits.

  • Reminding visitors that lottery games are based on chance, not skill.

  • Providing links to official support services for anyone who feels their play may be becoming a problem.

Tips for Playing Responsibly

  • Set a Budget: Decide in advance how much you can afford to spend on tickets and stick to it.

  • Play for Fun: Treat the lottery as entertainment, not an investment or income source.

  • Don’t Chase Losses: Accept that most draws will not result in a win and avoid spending more to recover losses.

  • Take Breaks: Step away from play regularly to keep it enjoyable.

Age Restrictions

All lottery participation in Australia is restricted to individuals aged 18 and over. Oz Pickz is intended for adult audiences only and does not encourage underage play.

Getting Help

If you feel your play is becoming harmful or difficult to control, contact one of these licensed support services:

  • Gambling Help Online: gamblinghelponline.org.au – Free, confidential 24/7 chat and email support.

  • Gambling Help Line: Call 1800 858 858 – Available 24/7 nationwide.

  • BetStop – National Self-Exclusion Register: betstop.gov.au – Voluntarily exclude yourself from all licensed online and phone wagering services in Australia.
