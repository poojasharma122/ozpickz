The Biggest Oz Pickz Wins in History

Since its launch in 1994, Oz Pickz has produced some incredible jackpot moments. At the top of the list is the record-breaking AUD 111 Million prize in November 2012, shared between four Division 1 winners across New South Wales, Queensland, Victoria, and Western Australia.

In August 2024, another headline-making draw saw AUD 100 Million split between three winners, while several AUD 70 Million jackpots have been awarded over the years.

These huge wins often follow multiple rollovers, and the media buzz they generate can lead to record ticket sales. While big jackpots are rare, they’re a reminder that the next draw could always be the one to change a life forever.
